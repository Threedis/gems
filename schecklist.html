Processing Pipeline

Field Supervisor
Completes the paper checklist on site during inspection.
Upload Filled Checklist
Scanned PDF is dropped into the intake panel on the right.
AI OCR Processing
Document pages are prepared and scanned for text regions.
Handwriting Recognition
Handwritten fields are converted into machine-readable text.
Extract Checklist Data
Names, dates, checklist items, and remarks are identified.
Validate Data
Low-confidence fields are flagged for a quick human check.
Populate Checklist Template
Extracted fields are mapped onto the standard template.
Generate Supervisor Summary
A dashboard summary is ready to review, export, or print.
This build runs entirely in your browser — extraction functions are wired up as placeholders (performOCR(), extractChecklist()) ready to connect to a real OCR/vision API.
